Beyond Strategic Planning

Objective:
To support educational leader and school system and their work on strategic planning and sustainable planning on issues of education.

Strategy:
To offer a menu of services within the expertise of the Cambridge Associates and their affiliation with their other job related activities.

Superintendent Search Assistance/Affiliation with BWP and Associates
Among the most important decisions that a school board makes is the selection of the superintendent. Dr. Harris and Dr. Castner also work with BWP and Associates, a national search firm that can provide you an experienced search team of experienced and dedicated consultants who are networked with educational leaders throughout the country. We offer a high level of expertise and extensive knowledge for recruiting exceptionally talented people.

Executive Coaching
Dr. Castner, Dr. Harris and Dr. Berlin have Executive Coaching experience with new superintendent and school system leaders . Executive coaching creates a professional alliance between a new superintendent and a successful veteran superintendent. Coaching can support a new superintendent to: successful action, personal accountability, organizational results, and capacity building with key system leaders to fulfill the school systems strategic goals.

School Board Academy(s)

New School Board member/superintendent orientation
A comprehensive session designed specifically to give new board members and superintendent an overview of the basics and boardmanship. In addition, the relationship of how School Board Policy can provide strategic direction for a school system will be explored.

Team of 8 Training (Texas)
Team of 8 training targets the effectiveness of the school board and superintendent relationship. The school board and the superintendent each have a distinct role to play in the education system within the school district, yet these distinct roles form an integral relationship referred to as a team. This training provides:

  • An overview of the components of a successful team
  • An assessment of the current status of the team’s effectiveness
  • Methods for enhancing the team’s strength based on its assessment
  • An opportunity to create dialogue among all members of the team
  • Opportunities to develop an approach to continue to build on the concepts learned during the training

Cambridge Associates are certified trainers in this process and have experience in the training of these teams as well as direct experience of how strategic thinking your team can be focused to policy driven learning outcomes for your students.
