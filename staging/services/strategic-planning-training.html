Strategic Planning Training

Developing the Strategic Plan...

This program will equip you to facilitate your organization’s strategic planning process; apply the Cambridge strategic planning discipline; establish locus of control within the system, rather than reacting to external factors; stimulate positive community involvement and support; and develop the organizational capability for continuous creation. Participants are provided with continuing support by Cambridge staff and are familiarized with the Strategic Planning Software. (5 Days)


This course is designed for school district administrators, especially district planners. The purpose is to train facilitators in both the process and discipline of strategic planning so they can facilitate the development and the realization of their district plan. The following subjects are covered:

  • Understanding strategic planning
  • Preliminary considerations for strategic planning
  • Community involvement
  • The Strategic Planning Discipline
  • The Strategic Planning Process
  • A realistic case study
  • Facilitator profiles
  • Communicating the strategic plan
  • Taking the strategic plan into action
  • Meeting accreditation requirements
  • Continuous assessment and renewal

Special Features:

  • This three-day intensive program comes complete with the critically acclaimed Internal Facilitator’s Reference Manual and Action Team Leader’s Guide
  • Three graduate credit hours are offered through the San Diego State University

Availability:

  • This training program is available, by application, in state partner and national locations
  • External facilitators for strategic planning are available from Cambridge on a contract basis
